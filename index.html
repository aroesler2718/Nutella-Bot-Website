Nutella Bot

Were you looking to buy the premium version of Nutella Bot or just add the free version of the bot to your server? If so, you've come to the right place.

Features

Dashboard

Nutella Bot has an online dashboard where users can do many things. From the dashboard users can create custom commands, edit the bot's prefix, enable, disable, and configure Nutella Bot's current commands.

Music

Nutella Bot can play any music that can be found on YouTube. You can search music by title or by entering a YouTube URL. Along with this, you can add songs to the queue, edit the queue, pause, seek, and do many other things.

Many Commands

You can find tons of useful commands in the advanced help menu of Nutella Bot. These commands can range from doing various calculations to searching images to moderating your server.
